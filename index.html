About Me

Hi, my name is Iris! I’m a software engineer who specializes in web development. I graduated from the University of Southern California with a major in Cognitive Science and a minor in Web Technologies and Applications.


I have experience designing and developing web applications using various technologies, from the fundamental trio of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to more robust frameworks and platforms like React, Next.js, Node, Express.js, PHP and Laravel. My passion extends beyond just aesthetics and I love seeking new and improved methods to write efficient and scalable code with the goal of creating seamless user experiences.


When I’m not at my computer, you can find me at the climbing gym projecting a V5, working on creating my perfect matcha bowl at my pottery wheel, or baking a bunch of sweets in the kitchen!
